"Touch the photos to learn more about me!"

It all started when I was a fully-grown little boy. My mother was an anthropomorphic, superintelligent hamster, while my father was a sapient elderberry bush. It was a happy, if unexciting childhood

Then, one day, while I was playing in the sandbox, I dug up an old treasure chest belonging to the infamous cyberpirate BitBeard. I cracked it open with my bare hands. Inside, was BitBeards' legendary golden gaming rig, thought to be lost forever. The moment I laid eyes on that solid-gold computer case and gem-encrusted keyboard, I knew that I was destined to become a programmer.

During my time as a programmer, I've become versed in many programming languages, not all of which are native to this universe!

I am proficient in the following human programming languages:

  • Python
  • C#
  • Ruby
  • Klingon
  • Elvish
  • Newspeak
  • Simlish
  • Horse
